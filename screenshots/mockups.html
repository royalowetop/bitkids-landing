BitKids App Store Screenshot Generator

To generate final screenshots:

  1. Replace the gray placeholder areas below with actual app screenshots (edit the HTML or use CSS background-image on .phone-placeholder).
  2. Scroll down to the Full-Size Export section where each card renders at the exact 1290x2796px dimensions.
  3. Open browser DevTools (F12), click the device toolbar, and set a custom resolution of 1290 x 2796.
  4. Isolate each export card (hide the others) and take a full-page screenshot, or use Ctrl+Shift+P > "Capture node screenshot" on each .export-card element.
  5. The resulting PNGs are ready to upload to App Store Connect and Google Play Console.

Quick tip

  • To insert a real screenshot, add style="background: url('your-image.png') center/cover no-repeat" to the .phone-placeholder div and remove its inner text.
