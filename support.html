Quick Help

How do I reset my password?
On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?" and enter the email address associated with your account. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes. If you don't see it, check your spam folder.
How do I add a child to my account?
Open the app and go to Settings, then tap "Add Child." You'll be asked to enter a name and create a PIN for the child's profile. Free accounts support one child; upgrade to a paid plan for unlimited children.
How do I fund my wallet?
Navigate to the Wallet tab and tap "Fund Wallet." You'll receive a Lightning invoice that you can pay from any Lightning-compatible Bitcoin wallet. The sats will appear in your balance once the payment confirms.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to Settings and tap the "Subscription" row. From there you can manage your plan. Since BitKids uses Lightning payments, there are no recurring charges — your subscription simply expires at the end of the billing period if you choose not to renew.
How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings and scroll to the bottom to find "Delete Account." This will permanently remove your account and all associated data, including child profiles and wallet history. Please make sure to withdraw any remaining sats before deleting. If you need help, email us at support@bitkids.app.
