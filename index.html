🚀 Getting Started

Up and running in 3 steps

No Bitcoin experience needed. We guide you every step of the way.

1

Create a Family Account

Sign up as a parent, then invite your child by sharing a simple code. Each child gets their own fun profile with a customizable avatar.

2

Fund & Set Rewards

Top up your Lightning wallet with sats, set a reward amount per lesson (e.g., 25 sats), and configure an allowance schedule. Takes under 2 minutes.

3

Kids Learn & Earn

Your child watches lessons, answers quiz questions, and earns real sats — deposited into their stash automatically. No parental action needed.

  • Configure weekly or monthly allowances that auto-pay
  • Require your approval for any purchase above your chosen limit
  • Get notified instantly when your child earns a reward or requests to spend
  • See a runway warning before your reward pool runs dry
  • Allocate sats across Spend, Save, and Reward buckets

Frequently asked questions

From the parent dashboard, tap "Fund Wallet" and scan a Lightning invoice with any Bitcoin wallet (Strike, Cash App, Blue Wallet, etc.). Sats arrive in seconds. You can top up as little or as often as you like.
BitKids uses a custodial model — your child's sats are held in your parent wallet and tracked virtually. This means you remain in full custody of the funds and can set spending limits. No private keys for children to lose.
BitKids is designed for children ages 6–16. The lessons are explained in simple, engaging language. Younger kids will need a little parental guidance; older teens can navigate the app independently.
